# Greeting

A greeting is a brief expression used to acknowledge someone's presence or initiate communication.

## Common Forms

- **"Hi"** - Informal, casual greeting
- **"Hello"** - Standard, versatile greeting appropriate for most contexts

## Usage Context

Greetings serve as social protocols to:
- Establish initial contact
- Signal friendliness and approachability
- Open channels of communication
