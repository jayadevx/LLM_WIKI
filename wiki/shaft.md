# Shaft

A shaft is a fundamental mechanical component used to transmit rotational motion and torque in mechanical systems.

## Applications

The shaft is a common component found in multiple machine systems:

- **[[Machine1]]** - Works with the [[Impeller]] to transfer energy
- **[[Machine2]]** - Paired with a [[Rotating Gear]] for power transmission

## Function

Shafts serve as the primary means of transmitting rotational motion between different parts of a mechanical system, enabling power transfer and coordinated movement.
